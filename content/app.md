## Description (listing)

Tired of traditional eBooks? LeafTok turns your reading experience into
something completely new. Our innovative technology breaks any book into
bite-sized cards, creating a unique and addictive way to read.

Key features: EPUB and PDF conversion into reading cards, vertical swipe
navigation, TikTok-style interface, text optimized for focused consumption.
