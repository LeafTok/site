Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to LeafTok. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Books and Content: The books you upload (PDFs, EPUBs) are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to sync them.
  • Reading Progress: Your reading history, bookmarks, and learning progress are stored locally on your device.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information (device type, OS version) for app optimization and bug fixes.
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance and user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Personalize your reading experience
  • Analyze app performance and fix bugs
  • Develop new features based on user behavior

4. Data Storage and Security

All your books and reading data are stored locally on your device. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

LeafTok may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them. We only share anonymous, aggregated data with these services.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Request data portability

7. Children's Privacy

LeafTok is designed for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
